Crowdfunding is a popular fundraising model that enables individuals and startups to solicit funds from donors. However, the industry is plagued by several challenges, including the lack of security and transparency, leading to potential fraud. Moreover, the issue of global contribution is often a challenge, where donors from different parts of the world face difficulties in contributing to projects they support. These issues create a significant barrier to entry for many entrepreneurs and limit the potential growth of crowdfunding as a viable funding model. Thus, there is a need for a modern, secure, and transparent crowdfunding platform that addresses these challenges to create a level playing field for all individuals and startups seeking funding.
